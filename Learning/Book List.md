## How to Study for ML Research (this matters more than books)

When you study, always ask:

- “What is the _object_ here?”
    
- “What is random, what is fixed?”
    
- “What changes if I add noise?”
    
- “What happens in high dimensions?”
    

If you can’t answer in words, you don’t understand it yet.
